About Us

From our humble beginnings in a car garage, We now an award-winning brick-and-mortar bike shop, with a world-renowned online presence. We are dedicated to providing the best products and service for you and your bike. Our staff is happy to help with everything from product suggestions to tips on some of the best local rides. We have a massive selection of bicycle components and accessories including packs, helmets, and lights. Our full-service repair shop can handle any and all repairs, tune-ups, or custom requests for any kind of bike.

While we specialize in mountain biking, our service and products extend far beyond that. We encourage you to stop in our shop, or strike up a chat with us online. We are here and eager to work with you - whether that be fixing a flat or building your next dream bike.

Our Mission

We want to amaze every bike lover with our creations. Our mission is to be the best dream bike builder in the world.
